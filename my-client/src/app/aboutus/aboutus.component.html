Crafting Pottery Excellence
Established in 2000, ClayCo has grown into a leading ceramics brand in Vietnam, expanding internationally in 2016. Renowned for its beautifully crafted pottery, ceramics, and plant accessories, ClayCo emphasizes artistry, aesthetics, and spiritual significance, continuously enhancing product quality over two decades.
