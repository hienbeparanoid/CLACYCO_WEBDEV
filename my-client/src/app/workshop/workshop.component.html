About our workshops

At Clayco, our workshops blend creativity and craftsmanship, inviting you to explore pottery inspired by nature’s beauty. Over the past year, we’ve hosted three seasonal workshops, bringing together over 200 pottery enthusiasts and resulting in 300 unique pieces. Each workshop is crafted to capture the essence of the season, guiding beginners and experts alike through every step, from molding clay to perfecting details.

This winter, join us for Icicle Inspirations, where we’ll transform clay into winter-inspired art, capturing the beauty of frost and snow. Reserve your spot today and let your creativity shine!
