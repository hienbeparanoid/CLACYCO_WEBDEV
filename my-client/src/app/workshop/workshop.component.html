About
our workshops
At Clayco, our workshops are where creativity meets craftsmanship, offering a hands-on experience that allows you to explore the art of pottery while connecting with nature. Over the past year, we’ve successfully hosted three seasonal workshops, each designed to capture the essence of its time of year and inspire our participants.

With more than 200 pottery enthusiasts joining us so far, our workshops have sparked creativity and resulted in over 300 unique ceramic pieces. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned potter, our workshops are tailored to guide you through every step of the process, from shaping the clay to adding the finishing touches.

Coming up this winter, we’re excited to present Icicle Inspirations: Crafting Winter in Clay. This special workshop will guide you through creating winter-themed ceramics, drawing inspiration from the beauty of frost and snow. Join us in capturing the magic of the season and transforming clay into something truly unique.
